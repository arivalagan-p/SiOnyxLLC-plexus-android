Note: This functionality is only supported by the Nightwave Digital Camera.

To open the Nightwave Digital Camera Wi-Fi settings menu, connect to the device via the app’s Home Screen. Once connected, you’ll gain access to manage all camera controls.

To connect to the camera, please follow these instructions:

  • Tap the NWD Camera icon on the Home Page to establish a connection with your Nightwave Digital Camera.
  • Enter the Wi-Fi password and press the connect button to initiate the connection.

To update your Camera’s Wi-Fi password, follow these steps:

  • Tap the NWD Camera icon on the Home Page and connect using your current password.
  • Long press on the connected camera icon on the Home Page.
  • Select the Wi-Fi Setting option from the bottom sheet.
  • Enter and confirm your new password, then click the Update button.
  • The camera will reboot after the password is changed, and the Wi-Fi connection will be lost.
  • Reconnect using the new password.

If you've forgotten the Wi-Fi password for your Nightwave Digital Camera, try the following:

Step 1:

  • Use a LAN cable to connect your Nightwave Digital Camera to your local network.
  • Open File Explorer on your computer and go to the Network section.
  • Look for the camera listed under a name starting with "CRV_".
  • Click the camera name to open its web interface in your browser.
  • On the web interface, find and select the "Factory" option to restore the camera’s default password as provided by SIONYX.

Alternatively, Step 2:

If Step 1 doesn’t work for you, please contact our customer support team. They’ll help guide you through resetting your password and regaining access to your device.
